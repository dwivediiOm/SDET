💡 Job Search Strategy Tips for Indore QA Professionals

🎯 Action Plan for 5+ Year SDET/QA Automation Professionals

1️⃣
Prioritise Impetus + InfoBeans first — These are Indore's strongest local employers for experienced QA automation. Both have dedicated QA Automation practices and hire 6–10 yr Java+Selenium profiles regularly. Apply directly at impetus.com/careers and infobeans.com/current-openings.
2️⃣
TCS is actively hiring in Indore for Selenium+Java — Naukri shows Selenium openings specifically tied to Indore/TCS. Apply via tcs.com/careers/india/lateral-hiring and also on Naukri with location filter "Indore" + "Selenium".
3️⃣
Add Playwright to your profile NOW — InfoBeans, Persistent, Hexaware, Nagarro, and GlobalLogic all prefer Selenium+Playwright dual-skill candidates and pay 15–25% more. Even 1–2 personal projects with Playwright can differentiate you.
4️⃣
Target remote roles for maximum pay — BrowserStack (~₹18–30 LPA), Thoughtworks (~₹18–35 LPA), GlobalLogic (~₹14–26 LPA) allow Indore residency. Remote income with Indore cost of living is the optimal financial strategy.
5️⃣
Use Instahyre and Naukri simultaneously — InfoBeans and NimbleWork actively post on Instahyre (instahyre.com/job-393440). Naukri shows ~98 Selenium jobs in Indore. Set job alerts on both platforms daily.
6️⃣
LinkedIn "Easy Apply" for MNC roles — Accenture, Cognizant, Capgemini, LTIMindtree all post on LinkedIn with Easy Apply. Search "QA automation Indore" or "SDET Indore" filtered to "Past Month" for the freshest openings.
7️⃣
Network at QA meetups — InfoBeans hosts BrowserStack QA meetups at Crystal IT Park. Attending these gets you direct contact with hiring managers. Check infobeans.ai/events for upcoming sessions.
8️⃣
Frame yourself as SDET, not "Tester" — Market data confirms SDET titles command higher pay. If your work involves framework development, CI/CD integration, or code reviews — use "SDET" or "Test Automation Engineer" in your profile title.
